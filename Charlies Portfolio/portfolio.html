Hey I'm Charlie, I am an emerging artist currently living in Kansas city Missouri

I like animation and art. The reason I want to be an animator is to tell my stories. Ever since I was little, I’ve wanted to share my stories and imagination with the world, and help other to share their creations. Animation is the best medium for me to show my stories, combining my character design abilities and with my natural talent for designing entertaining and thought provoking stories. I also have a profound gift for movement and expressions. I have been working on my children’s show, Last Thursday, since I was fourteen years old.
